Paul Bunyan & Babe the Blue Ox


Paul Bunyan and Babe the Blue Ox, was a menecing serial murder duo, first appearing after The Transfer Act of 1905 to the Bureau of Forestry which introduced the United States Forest Service. Paul Bunyan began impersonating a traveling forestry agent for the USFS, he would move between villages pretending to survey the lands.
